Quest Color System

Color is to be used sparingly and meaningfully.

Overview

The general principles of using color should follow natural patterns. For example, backgrounds should be darker than foregrounds to simulate the representation of dimensional space. If something is placed on top of another item, it will naturally be lighter in color due to the shadow and the raised item receiving more light.

Color should be meaningful and used to draw attention to important elements. More about this below.
